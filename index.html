Aloha & Welcome!

The North Shore Country Market is more than just a market; it's a gathering place fueled by community spirit and the bounty of our `aina (land). As a non-profit organization serving the Haleiwa area, our mission is to support local farmers, talented artisans, and food creators while providing residents and visitors with access to fresh, locally-sourced goods.

Founded with a passion for sustainability and connection, we strive to cultivate a vibrant atmosphere where the spirit of Aloha flourishes. Every purchase you make directly supports our local vendors and our non-profit initiatives.

What You'll Find

Discover the diverse offerings from our passionate local vendors:

Local & Organic Produce

Taste the freshness! Find seasonal fruits, vegetables, and herbs grown right here on Oahu's North Shore.

Artisan Crafts & Gifts

Unique, handcrafted items from local artists – jewelry, clothing, home decor, and more reflecting Hawaiian culture.

Delicious Prepared Foods

Grab a tasty bite! Enjoy local favorites, baked goods, refreshing drinks, and plate lunches made with aloha.

Tropical Flowers & Plants

Brighten your home with beautiful tropical flowers, leis, and locally grown plants.

Vendor offerings may vary weekly. Come see what treasures await!

Market Tips

  • Bring reusable bags!
  • Cash is helpful, though some vendors accept cards.

  • Enjoy the live music (when available)!
